Some common questions
were often asked

My flowers are falling off or dying?

Ensure proper watering and light conditions. Consider trimming dead blooms and checking for pests.

What causes leaves to become pale?

Pale leaves can result from insufficient sunlight or nutrient deficiencies. Consider adjusting light exposure and fertilizing appropriately.

What causes brown crispy leaves?

Brown crispy leaves may indicate underwatering, excessive sunlight, or low humidity levels. Adjust watering frequency and monitor environmental conditions.

How do i choose a plant?

Consider your space, light conditions, and care commitment. Research plants suitable for your environment and preferences before selecting.

How do I change the pots?

Carefully transplant your plant into a slightly larger pot with fresh soil. Ensure proper drainage and water the plant after repotting.

Why are gnats flying around my plant?

Gnats are attracted to moist soil. Allow soil to dry between waterings and consider using sticky traps to manage their population.
